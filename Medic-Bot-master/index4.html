who are we?

We are a team of learners and aspiring developers who provide cutting-edged technology (AI powered Chat bot) used to provide medical assistance and guide related to some disease to the population of Buea, Southwest region Cameroon.

Our mission is to create an awareness about some diseases which may be harmful to human health and may be taken likely.

Our vision is to provide medical assistance to the population of Buea (especially the Molyko population) through the use of our system.

THE PODUCT( SOFTWARE)


what good is our software?

With this product, the user is able to get important information about some diseases like;
» HIV
» Kidney related diseases
» Diabetes
» Cardiovascular diseases
» Hypertension and much more

The software permits the user to get in contact with particular health specialists and even hospitals by providing him/her with the specialist's contact (email, WhatsApp number, Tel) as well as the address and location of health centers.

This software may recommend some healthy life style practices to users and provides an interface where the user can write some comments and reviews about his experience after using the product.

Our team is made up of passionate, dedicated, serviceable and reliable developers (Front-End, Back-End, and Designers) whose main aim is to ensure customer (user, employer) satisfaction.
